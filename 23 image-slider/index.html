Daisy
As a flower, daisies symbolize innocence, purity, loyalty, patience, and simplicity.
Tulip
The most known meaning of tulips is perfect and deep love. As tulips are a classic flower that has been loved by many for centuries they have been attached with the meaning of love.
Lotus
It is known as the flower of the dawn and is the symbol of purity, commitment and optimism for the future.

Sunflower
Sunflowers are useful plants with large flower heads, or blooms. They are named for the way they turn their blooms from east to west to follow the Sun.
Rose
A red rose is a symbol of love, however, a deep red rose is a symbol of regret and sorrow.
